Frequently Asked Questions

This application is an Image-Based Diagnostic Assistant designed to help with the diagnosis of brain MRI images using advanced machine learning models.

To upload an image, navigate to the Upload page, select your image file, and click the "Upload" button. The system will process the image and provide a diagnosis.

The system supports commonly used image formats such as JPG, JPEG, and PNG.
